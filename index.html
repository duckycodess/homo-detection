Between Inclusion and Prejudice: Examining LGBTQ+ Discourse and Homophobia in Filipino Reddit Spaces

This initiative aims to explore the discourse surrounding LGBTQ+ issues and homophobia within Filipino Reddit communities. Our project analyzes how these discussions unfold, investigates the prevalence of homophobic sentiment, and examines the correlation between sentiment and engagement within these spaces. By examining the dynamics of LGBTQ+ discourse, we aim to uncover patterns that could inform advocacy, inclusivity initiatives, and strategies to combat homophobia online.

Our Research Focus

Research Questions

  • Can Reddit posts containing LGBTQ+ related terms be automatically classified as homophobic or non-homophobic using NLP techniques?
  • What is the overall sentiment distribution in Reddit posts containing LGBTQ+ related terms?
  • What is the relationship between sentiment and engagement in posts using LGBTQ+ related terms?

Hypotheses

  • Homophobic and non-homophobic posts exhibit significant differences in their language features.
  • Negative sentiment is more prevalent than positive or neutral sentiment in LGBTQ+ Reddit discourse.
  • Posts with negative or homophobic sentiment receive higher engagement (comments/upvotes) than other posts.

Methods

We apply natural language processing techniques for classification and sentiment analysis, then use statistical tests to examine the relationships between identified variables (e.g. ANOVA, chi-square).

Solution

Our findings aim to inform advocacy and moderation strategies to foster inclusivity and combat homophobia within Filipino Reddit communities.

Data

Our dataset consists of Reddit posts from Filipino communities that mention LGBTQ+ related terms. The analysis covers posts from a variety of discussions, including both homophobic and inclusive content.
